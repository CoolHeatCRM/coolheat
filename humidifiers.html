Why would I want to Install a Humidifier?

For many reasons:

Lower Energy Costs

Since the air in your home is always trying to reach its saturation point it will absorb water wherever it can; from the bodies of everyone in your home, your furniture and even your house plants. As a result your skin, throat and nasal passages dry out, leaving you more susceptible to physical discomfort, colds, flu and even infection. Installing a Furnace Humidifier can expand your control over your Comfort Systems preventing dry, ichty skin while reducing the cost of your energy bills. Ask a CoolHeat Technician how you can save money by installing a Humidifier.

Control Humidity

Over time dry air creates gaps in window & door frames letting cold outdoor air in. You then need to to turn up the heat which increases the cost of your heating bills! Controlled humidity allows you the luxury of dialing the thermostat back & reducing annual heating bills. For example, 68°F at 40% relative humidity feels just as warm as 74°F at 20% humidity. Setting your thermostat back by as little as 3°F can reduce annual heating bills by as much as 5%. Speak with a CoolHeat Comfort Technician about Himudity Control over your Comfort Systems.

Protect your Valuables

If dry air causes dry, itchy skin imagine what it does to expensive woodwork and paintings. It cracks floors, artwork, furnishings and can cause harm to expensive musical instruments like pianos, violins, and guitars. Learn what it means to Control your Relative Humidity and prevent damage to your valuables.

Prevent Static

Annoying static electricity (caused by dry air) can damage computers, VCR’s and other electronic equipment, requiring expensive repair. Ask one of our Expert Comfort Technicians about proper Humidity Protection.

What is Humidity Load?

Humidity Load (aka -Gallons Per Day) represents the amount of water a humidifier must process to properly humidify your home. Humidity load has a direct impact on the life of a humidifier, and a steam cylinder. When properly sized to a home, a humidifier will rarely be required to run at full capacity, extending the life of the humidifier. What are the benefits of installing a Humidifier?

Most of the tissue in our bodies is composed of water.
