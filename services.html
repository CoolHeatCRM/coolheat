What are the Benefits of Yearly Servicing?

The benefits of servicing your Comfort System is to maximize the longevity of your System and to ensure that its running properly and safely. Cleaning and Maintaining your Comfort System will also improve its efficiency and lower your overall energy cost.

Do you have yearly Maintenance Plans?

The most important thing you can do to improve the life of your furnace is to properly service it with yearly maintenance. We offer a variety of Service and Maintenance Plans starting at $119/year. Speak with a CoolHeat Comfort Technician for further details.
