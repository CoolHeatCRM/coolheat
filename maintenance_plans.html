Why CoolHeat Comfort?

Our family run business offers exceptional customer service as well as quality brand name products. Our Comfort Technicians will guide you through the whole process. From assessing your Comfort needs; to installing your Comfort System; to registering your warranty. Your Comfort Specialist will instruct you on proper Service & Maintenance of your equipment so it'll run at optimum efficiency for years to come. When you choose CoolHeat we are there with you for the life of your Comfort System and beyond.

What is done during a full service and cleaning?

When completing a full Maintenance of your HVAC System Coolheat Comfort will replace your current filter and give you a replacement filter to be changed out every 6 months. Our Comfort Technicians will complete a Full Maintenance Precision Tune-up; Cleaning & Safety Inspection of all units; and Verify all Pressures & Measurements. While we're there we'll also take a quick look at your other CoolHeat Comfort Systems.

What type of Financing Options are available?

Buying a Brand New Comfort System can be an expensive, upfront cost. CoolHeat offers Financing and Rental options. Your CoolHeat Comfort Technician will guide you through choosing the best value for your home.

What is your 10-Year Warranty?

Coolheat offers the best warranties in the HVAC business. Our 10 year Parts & Labour Warranty will have you sleeping comfortably for years to come. Our professionally trained Comfort Technicians will guide you through other warranties available for your Comfort Systems during your free energy consultation.

What rebates are available?

Rebates for a new Home Comfort System depend on the type of System you're installing. By installing a new Comfort System you can qualify for a rebate from the Ontario Power Authority. Please contact us for a free energy consultation for more information.
